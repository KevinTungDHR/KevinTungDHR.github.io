About Me

I am a film and television creative turned software engineer with a passion for problem solving and collaborating with other people. My time in film and television has given me a keen eye for detail that translates to my ability to focus on how small things can affect the larger user experience. In spring 2022, I attended a highly intensive coding bootcamp called App Academy, spending 90-100 hours a week learning full-stack development over a span of four months. I am experienced working across the stack using technologies such as JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, React/Redux, Express, Node, MongoDB, and SQL. Outside of software development, I love playing modern board games such as New Frontiers, Food Chain Magnate and Spirit Island. In addition, I enjoy reading about chess and attempting (and often failing) to gain Elo rating on chess.com.
